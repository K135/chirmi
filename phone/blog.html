AN ENGLISH VINTAGE THEMED WEDDING THEME IN BANGALORE

Attending an english countryside wedding in the heart of Bangalore at the Taj Westend was a quite a treat for the guests of Mr. & Mrs. Poddar. Our first meeting with the Poddar family and we were told that they are looking for wedding planners in Bangalore who have fresh ideas for an English theme vintage country side wedding décor and who understand the essence and who can do a brilliant execution for their daughter's wedding reception. We knew we were up for the challenge as their wedding planners and their wedding decorators.

 

Taj Westend opened its vast and expansive gardens for the reception of the gorgeous couple. They were extremely accommodating for the Chirmi wedding team of wedding planners.

 

A vintage theme reception can be justified only if there are hand crafted elements. With a very dedicated and well informed wedding planners team at Chirmi events, we set out to look for the best of the best for this Job. Right from hand-painted welcome boards to the dreamy entrance with lace arches and pathway filled up with pearls, fairy lights, white and pink flowers, we made sure every wedding decoration detail is taken care off.

 

Add to that a wooden swing and it sets a perfect spot to celebrate love and be captured as a memory for life. Hand crafted wooden crates with pearls and floral décor to rustic center pieces, old type writers, clocks and gramophones to mason jars and flower urns placed all around the lawn are a few examples to show at what length we go to make your wedding planning and wedding décor dream come true.

 

Would a union like this be complete without the blessings from the loved ones...we dint think so. A vintage cart with writing cards to leave your message was the perfect solution. And for the ones who enjoy a drink to add to their celebration, a huge cut out of the word 'BAR' was suspended. The band was given the perfect inspiration for their classic English and Hindi songs with old vinyl records suspended around the tent. A gradient shade of white to pink backdrop looked spectacular and went perfect with the outfits of the couple. The final touch which subtly did the job of creating the right ambience for this evening was the various kinds of Lights. We had every element that would transport you to a European country side, and as wedding planners we just it right and enjoyed every bit of this.

 

All of this would not be possible without a very warm and welcoming family of the Poddar's from Aashirwad pipes. We'd like to thank you for giving us this opportunity and keeping trust in us as their wedding planners.

 

We wish them all the love and luck.

 

 

A POLKA DOTS ENGAGEMENT THEME TO REMEMBER

Sharanya & Rakshith wanted a brunch engagement and as their wedding planners & wedding decorators we at Chirmi weddings enjoyed doing every bit of it. Polka dots never get old and polka dot theme wedding décor theme is always a mark of style & sophistication. Polka dots décor is very experimental and not many choose this décor. Though sharanya was very confident about this and that's why it was a hit.

 

Black-white and a tinge of pink was her wedding décor theme and it came out just perfect. With showing samples of how their engagement would look to booking her caterer and helping her choose her outfit to customizing their dance floor. It was absolute fun.

 

Open venues are a boon to wedding planners in Bangalore. It gets so much more fun to work and do any kind of wedding decorations and we as wedding planners always enjoy open spaces. Once such event venue is Bangalore is Temple tree farm in sahakarnagar. Simple, clean and open.

 

We would like to thank them for giving us this opportunity. We wish them the very best.
